Chords

Simply put this is a collection of notes which sound good together.

Think of notes like ingredients that when added together forms a wonderful and tasty dish(chords)

The chords, similar to notes, run between A to G and having a sharp between each, but this is not always as so

It goes as so; A, A# ,B ,c ,c# ,D ,D# ,E ,F ,F# ,G and lastly G#
